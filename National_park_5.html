Holmes Jungle Nature Park

Description: Holmes Jungle Nature Park protects large numbers of birds, mammals and reptiles that seek refuge in the cool monsoon rainforest. The hilltop area overlooks the wetlands and surrounding bushland and is an ideal spot for a picnic. Cyclists and horse riders are welcome in the park on a separate shared track.

Address: Holmes NT 0812

Open hours: Everyday 8am-6pm
