Woolworths (Darwin City)

Description: Woolworths (colloquially "Woolies") is an Australian chain of supermarkets and grocery stores owned by Woolworths Group. Founded in 1924, Woolworths along with Coles forms a near-duopoly of Australian supermarkets, accounting for about 80% of the Australian market.

Address: Cavenagh St & Whitfield Street, Darwin City NT 0800

Open hours: Everyday 6am-10pm
