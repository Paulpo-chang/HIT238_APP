Casuarina Square

Description: The largest shopping centre in the Northern Territory, is located in Casuarina in Darwin's northern suburbs. The shopping centre is built to the building code for Tropical Cyclones, due to cyclones that sweep through the area such as Cyclone Tracy in 1974.

Address: 247 Trower Rd, Casuarina NT 0810

Open hours: Mon-Thu: 9am-5:30pm, Fri: 9am-9pm, Sat: 9am-5pm, Sun: 10am-3pm
