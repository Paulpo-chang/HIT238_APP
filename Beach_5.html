Nightcliff Beach

Description: The beach is moderately steep and narrow at high tide, while 200 m wide sand and some rock flats are exposed at low tide. It is separated from its neighbouring beach by a 200 m wide 10 m high headland, with a public swimming pool and park located on the headland.

Address: Nightcliff Beach, 244 Casuarina Dr, Nightcliff NT 0810

Open hours: All day
