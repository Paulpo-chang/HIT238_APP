Mary River National Park

Description: Anyone who's headed to the Northern Territory to catch a barramundi has heard of Shady Camp in Mary River National Park. Apart from spectacular fishing, Mary River is popular for four-wheel driving, bushwalking and birdwatching. It's a must-see park for any nature lover.

Address: Mary River National Park, Arnhem Hwy, Marrakai NT 0822
